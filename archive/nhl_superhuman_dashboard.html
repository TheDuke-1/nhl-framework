üß† How The Superhuman Model Works

The NHL Superhuman Prediction System uses an ensemble of machine learning models trained on 15 years of historical data (2010-2024) with 462 team-seasons and 225 playoff series.

Model Components:

  • Logistic Regression - Playoff probability classification
  • Gradient Boosting - Cup prediction with feature importance
  • Neural Network - Deep pattern recognition
  • Monte Carlo Simulation - 10,000 bracket simulations per prediction
  • Playoff Series Model - Round-specific upset rate adjustments
  • Isotonic Calibration - Probability accuracy tuning

Key Innovation: Our playoff series model discovered that Conference Finals are essentially 50-50 regardless of seeding - a critical insight the basic models miss.

Proven Accuracy: 30.8% Top-1 accuracy (9.8x better than random), 46.2% Top-3 accuracy, 84.6% Top-10 accuracy.
